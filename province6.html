Rara Lake
Rara Lake, the largest lake in Nepal, is a serene and stunning destination located in Mugu district. Surrounded by the Rara National Park, it's perfect for peaceful getaways and nature adventures.
Shey Phoksundo National Park
Shey Phoksundo National Park is home to the spectacular turquoise Phoksundo Lake and rare wildlife like snow leopards. It's the largest national park in Nepal and a top destination for trekkers.
Kankrebihar Temple
Located in Surkhet, Kankrebihar is a historic Hindu-Buddhist temple built in the 12th century. Its unique stone architecture and spiritual ambiance make it a significant cultural landmark.
