Lumbini
Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha, is a sacred pilgrimage site in Nepal. It features the Maya Devi Temple, ancient ruins, and a peaceful garden that attracts visitors from around the world seeking spiritual serenity.
Rani Mahal
Known as the "Taj Mahal of Nepal," Rani Mahal is a stunning historical palace located on the banks of the Kali Gandaki River. Built in the 1890s, it symbolizes love and devotion and offers a mesmerizing view of nature and architecture.
